¹For Reference Only

Brand Identity Exhibition Design

A curation of 49 works from current and previous students of the RISD Graphic Design Graduate Program at the Sol Koffler Gallery in Providence, RI. ¹For Reference Only invited visitors to consider how design is encountered, remembered, and recontextualized over time, asking whether reference itself is a form of life or a kind of slow erasure. Softness

Art Direction Editorial Design

This publication seeks to question softness and its various dimensions which shape our sociopolitical and cultural landscapes. Softness is not about fragility, but about strength in vulnerability, compassion, and empathy. It is a quiet form of resistance, a deliberate choice to remain open and tender, to care. **V.2 is coming soon!

Oblique Act

Creative Coding

A tool that generates a random question prompt and pairs it with a physical action instruction — print it, and do it. This action prompt tool inspired by Brian Eno's Oblique Strategies — but instead of redirecting your thinking, it redirects your body. View project here

The book itself functions as a portal with excerpts drawn from diverse fields, voices, cultures, times, and places that come together as fragments.
